# Outreach Templates

## Email Templates

### Tier 1: Direct, Evidence-Based

These prospects have confirmed tech stack and strong fit. The email should demonstrate you've done your homework.

**Structure:**
```
Subject: [Specific reference to their tech/pain] — [value prop]

Hi [First Name],

[1 sentence showing you know their company and role]

[1-2 sentences referencing the specific evidence: their case study, job posting,
or tech blog. Show you understand their current architecture and its limitations.]

[1-2 sentences about how Zilliz/Milvus addresses their specific situation.
Reference a comparable customer if relevant.]

[Clear CTA: specific next step, not vague "let's chat"]

Best,
[Name]
```

**Example Subject Lines (adapt to each prospect):**
- "Scaling vector search beyond OpenSearch kNN at [Company]"
- "Re: your [Job Title] posting — vector search at [X] scale"
- "How [Similar Customer] solved [specific problem] you mentioned in [source]"
- "[Company]'s [product feature] + purpose-built vector search"

**Example CTA options:**
- "Would a 30-min architecture walkthrough comparing your current OpenSearch kNN setup vs. Zilliz Cloud be useful?"
- "Happy to share benchmarks from [similar customer] who migrated from [their current infra]. Worth 20 minutes?"
- "We put together a technical comparison for [their use case]. Can I send it over?"

---

### Tier 2: Consultative, Value-First

Medium evidence — lead with industry insight, not product pitch.

**Structure:**
```
Subject: [Industry trend or insight] — thoughts for [Company]

Hi [First Name],

[1 sentence about an industry trend relevant to their business]

[2-3 sentences sharing a genuine insight about how companies in their space
are adopting vector search. Be specific about the use case — not generic AI hype.]

[1 sentence connecting this to their situation, based on what you've researched]

[Soft CTA: offer value, not a demo]

Best,
[Name]
```

**Example Subject Lines:**
- "How [industry] companies are upgrading from keyword to semantic search"
- "Vector search trends in [industry] — [region] landscape"
- "Quick question about [Company]'s AI roadmap"

**Example CTA options:**
- "We published a technical brief on vector search for [industry] — want me to send it?"
- "Would a 15-min call to share what we're seeing across [industry] be interesting?"
- "I'm hosting a small roundtable with [industry] engineering leaders next month. Interested?"

---

### Tier 3: Educational, Long-Game

Low evidence, mostly inferred fit. Build awareness over time.

**Structure:**
```
Subject: [Broad industry trend] for [Company/industry]

Hi [First Name],

[1-2 sentences about a broad technology trend relevant to their business]

[1-2 sentences about a specific customer story that might resonate,
without heavy pitching]

[Light CTA: content share or event invite]

Best,
[Name]
```

**Example Subject Lines:**
- "AI-powered [use case] in [industry] — case study"
- "How [reference customer] handles [relevant challenge]"
- "[Industry] + vector search: what's changing in 2026"

**Example CTA options:**
- "We just published a case study on [reference customer] — thought it might be relevant to [Company]'s direction"
- "We're running a webinar on [topic] next month with engineers from [reference customers]. Want an invite?"
- "Happy to share our [industry] whitepaper if useful for your team's planning"

## LinkedIn Messages

### Connection Request Templates (300 char max)

**Tier 1:**
```
Hi [Name], I saw [specific reference — their case study, talk, or blog post]
and was impressed by [Company]'s approach to [topic]. I work on vector search
infra at Zilliz — would love to exchange ideas on [their specific challenge].
```

**Tier 2:**
```
Hi [Name], I work with [industry] companies on search infrastructure at Zilliz.
Noticed [Company] is [doing something relevant — launching AI features, scaling, etc.].
Would love to connect and share what we're seeing in the space.
```

**Tier 3:**
```
Hi [Name], I help [industry] companies with AI search infrastructure.
Saw [something specific about them or their company].
Would love to connect — happy to share relevant insights from the space.
```

### Follow-Up Message Templates (after connection accepted)

**Tier 1:**
```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]!

I mentioned [their case study/blog/talk] — we work with companies like
[reference customer] on similar challenges around [specific use case].

I put together a quick technical comparison that might be relevant to
[Company]'s setup. Happy to share if useful?
```

**Tier 2:**
```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]!

I've been following [Company]'s [recent initiative or product]. We recently
helped [reference customer] in [adjacent industry] solve a similar challenge
with purpose-built vector search.

Would a quick 15-min call to share what we've learned be useful?
```

**Tier 3:**
```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]!

We just published [relevant content piece — blog, whitepaper, case study]
that covers how [industry] companies are using vector search for [use case].
Thought it might be interesting for your team at [Company].

Happy to share the link!
```

## Personalization Guidance

The templates above are structures, not scripts. Every message should be customized with:

1. **The prospect's name and company** (obviously)
2. **Something specific from your research** — a quote from their case study, a detail from their job posting, a feature they launched
3. **A relevant reference customer** — ideally in the same industry or with a similar use case
4. **A specific CTA** — not "let's chat sometime" but "would a 30-min architecture comparison be useful?"

The goal is for the recipient to think "this person actually knows what we do" — not "this is a template."
